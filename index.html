What We Do & How It Works

Trust V Care provides dependable support for elderly parents, homes, and everyday responsibilities in Kerala and Tamil Nadu for families living abroad. Our goal is to ensure your loved ones receive the care and assistance they need while giving you complete peace of mind.

  1. Choose a Plan: Select a service plan that fits your family’s needs—Silver, Gold, or Platinum.
  2. Personalized Setup: Our team connects with you to understand your parents’ needs and home requirements, then creates a personalized support plan.
  3. Regular Visits & Assistance: Trained service executives visit your parents and property, assisting with errands, bills, groceries, hospital visits, and other needs.
  4. Updates & Transparency: After every visit, we provide updates, photos, and reports so you always know how your loved ones are doing.
  5. Ongoing Support: Our team is available for additional help whenever needed, including emergencies, property maintenance, and special requests.

Safety & Trust

What measures are taken to ensure the safety of my elderly loved ones?

At Trust V Care, your loved ones’ safety is our top priority:

  • Every team member undergoes strict background checks, including identity verification, criminal record screening, and reference verification.
  • Service executives are trained professionals selected for reliability, empathy, and integrity.
  • They carry company-issued ID, wear identifiable uniforms, and follow strict service protocols during every visit.
  • Visits are pre-scheduled, digitally logged, and monitored, ensuring transparency and accountability.
  • Families receive regular updates with photos and reports, and our operations team supervises services to maintain consistent quality.
  • Immediate alerts are sent to families if any concerns arise, ensuring 24/7 peace of mind.
