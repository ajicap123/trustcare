Peace of Mind for Families Abroad

You care deeply about your parents and loved ones. But distance can make it difficult to support them with everyday needs.

TrustCare Global bridges that gap. We provide reliable family and property support services in Kerala and Tamil Nadu, coordinated by you and delivered by our trusted local team.

Our services empower older parents to live more independently by assisting with daily tasks, errands, and appointments—reducing reliance on neighbours or friends. Families abroad get true peace of mind knowing a dependable team is on the ground.

What We Do & How It Works

  1. Choose a Plan: Silver, Gold, or Platinum, based on your family’s needs.
  2. Personalized Setup: Our team understands your parents’ needs and home requirements to create a tailored plan.
  3. Regular Visits & Assistance: Grocery, medicine, bill payments, hospital visits, and more.
  4. Updates & Transparency: Photos, reports, and digital logs after every visit.
  5. Ongoing Support: Emergency assistance, property maintenance, and special requests whenever needed.
